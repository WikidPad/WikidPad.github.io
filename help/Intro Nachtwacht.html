De compagnie van Frans Banning Cocq

Place:
Rijksmuseum, Amsterdam
Grote Sael van de Cleveniersdoelen
(Doelenzaal in the Kloveniersburgwal)

The Night Watch, the most famous painting in the Rijksmuseum, actually has another title: the 'Company of Frans Banning Cocq and Willem van Ruytenburch'. The picture is a militia painting: a group portrait of a division of the civic guard. Read more in Rembrandt 400 »
