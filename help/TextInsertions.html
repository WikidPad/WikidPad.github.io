General

In wikidpad the posibility exists, to insert the contents of one or more pages into a third page. To do this, you use the [:page: pagename] directive on the inserting page on the position within the text where you want the page to be inserted. This facility is explored in a number of subject specific pages.

Remarks

  • When a page is inserted, 100% of that page's text is inserted, including page title, page formatting attributes, etc.
  • Page formatting and other attributes on the inserted page for the larger part have n√≥ influence on the inserting page.
  • Headers in an inserted page do not become part of the inserting page's table of contents ([:toc:])
