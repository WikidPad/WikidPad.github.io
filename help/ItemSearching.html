Item Searching

Back
WikidPad offers several powerfull facilities to search either the current page or the whole wiki. The facilities to search a page in preview are not equal to those of the editor.
  1. Search current page
    • Preview: Find dialog [1]
    • Editor: Incremental search
    • Editor: Find & Replace dialog.
  2. Search whole wiki.
    • Fast search in the toolbar
    • Full search dialog

[1] The find dialog that is started depends on the used preview renderer: internal or external.
